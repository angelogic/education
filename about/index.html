    QA Testing

  • Training Introduction

    Quality Assurance (QA) in software development is the continuous monitoring of the complete software development cycle to improve the performance of the software product and to see that the final results are same as the expected results. With the help of QA and Testing tools techniques we will ensure that software product is a quality and bug free product. Online QA training course will make you understand the concepts of different testing environments. The software quality assurance online training courses trains you to use automation testing tools and manual testing tools.

  • Career Scope

    In the early days even testing was performed by the developers only, with the increasing demand of the quality of the product and to avoid the failures of the product after manufacturing it, which can lead to great money loss as well as life loss in some cases. In order to avoid all these failures, they have decided to perform testing from the scratch level. With this decision Software testing has become a profession. The need for QA testers will be always there as long as the programs are developed, so the scope for Software Testing is huge in the market. QA will be a good career prospective for the people who are looking for better career opportunities.
